FAQ

Common questions.

What exactly do I get in the $297 trial?

Three deliverables: (1) a Marketing Audit PDF covering your funnel, copy, email strategy, ads, and competitors — with a prioritized fix list, (2) a GEO Visibility PDF with your AI search score for ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews — with specific fixes ranked by impact, and (3) a qualified lead list with personalized outreach messages for every Tier A lead. Delivered within 48–72 hours. The trial is diagnosis — you get the full picture of what's broken and exactly what to fix.

When does the fixing actually start?

Month 2 — your first monthly payment. The trial gives you the diagnosis and the fix list. Starting month 2, we deliver ready-to-implement assets every month based on that list: exact schema JSON-LD code, a complete llms.txt file, rewritten copy blocks, improved email sequences, and updated CTAs. You copy-paste them into your site or ESP — no developer or agency access needed. Most fixes take under 10 minutes to implement on your end.

Do I need to give you access to my website?

No. We deliver the assets ready to use — the exact code, copy, and files you need. You paste the schema markup into your site, upload the llms.txt, swap in the rewritten copy. Everything is built so your team can implement it without any technical help. If something needs a developer, we flag it and provide the exact spec.

Where do the 100 leads in the trial come from?

If you have Apollo or Sales Navigator (Offer A), you do a quick export — takes about 5 minutes — and send us the CSV. We run it through the qualification engine. If you don't have a lead tool (Offer B), you describe your ideal customer on the onboarding call and we pull the leads from our own sourcing tools. Either way, you get a scored, tiered lead list with messages ready to send.

How many leads do I get each month after the trial?

Up to 200 leads per month — double the trial volume. Offer A: you export from Apollo or Sales Nav and send us the CSV. Offer B: we source them for you. Every lead is scored, tiered A/B/C, and every Tier A lead gets a personalized outreach message written and ready to send.

What does the GEO audit actually measure?

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) measures how visible your business is to AI search engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. These now drive a significant share of B2B research traffic and most companies are invisible to them. We score your site 0–100 across citability, technical access, schema markup, brand mentions, and content structure. The monthly score report tracks your number improving month over month.

What's the Python script bonus?

If you decide not to continue after the trial, we send you the live signal qualification script we use — a Python tool that scores any lead list against 5 buying signals (company size, SDR/BDR hiring activity, recent funding, industry fit, and title match) and returns a Tier A / B / C ranked CSV. It runs on your machine and uses SerpAPI for the live signals (~$50/month for typical usage). It's the actual tool we built and use for every client — yours to run yourself if you'd rather go it alone.

Is there a contract or setup fee?

No to both. The trial is $297 flat — no setup fee, no commitment. If you continue to monthly, it's $750/month with no setup fee ever. The trial does all the onboarding work. Cancel anytime.
