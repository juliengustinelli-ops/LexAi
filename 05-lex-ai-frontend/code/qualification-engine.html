FAQ

Common questions.

What do I actually get in the $497 trial?

Three deliverables: a full AI Marketing Audit PDF (47 checks, every finding has a specific fix), a full GEO Visibility PDF (AI search score across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews with exact fixes), and a qualified lead list with personalized outreach messages for every Tier A lead. Delivered within 48–72 hours. The trial is the diagnosis — you get the full picture of what's broken and what to fix first.

When does the fixing actually start?

Month 2 — your first monthly payment. The trial gives you the diagnosis and the full fix list. Starting month 2, we deliver ready-to-implement assets every month: schema JSON-LD code, llms.txt updates, rewritten copy blocks, email sequences, and updated CTAs. You copy-paste. Most fixes take under 10 minutes to implement with no developer needed.

Where do the leads come from?

If you have Apollo or Sales Navigator, you do a quick export — about 5 minutes — and send us the CSV. We run it through the qualification engine. If you don't have a lead tool, describe your ideal customer on the onboarding call and we pull the leads ourselves. Either way, you get a scored, tiered list with personalized messages ready to send.

What's in the weekly SalesIQ brief?

Every Monday before 9AM: 20 signal-scored prospects ranked by buying readiness. The top 2 get full intelligence packs — org chart, decision maker map, BANT/MEDDIC scorecard, live buying signal analysis, and ready-to-send outreach copy. The next 3 get 1-page snapshot briefs. The remaining 15 are scored and tagged with their active signals. It's built for teams who want to show up Monday and know exactly who to call. Included in the Full Stack plan.

Do I need to give you access to my website?

No. We deliver everything as ready-to-use files and copy — exact schema code, updated llms.txt, rewritten copy blocks. You or your team pastes them in. If anything requires developer work, we flag it and provide the exact spec. No CMS access, no credentials, nothing.

What happens if I don't continue after the trial?

You keep everything — both audit PDFs, your qualified lead list, and your personalized outreach messages. We also send you our live-signal lead qualification script (Python) — the same tool we use to score leads against hiring activity and funding signals — so you can run it yourself going forward. No strings, no expiring assets.
