What We Do

LexAi builds custom AI-powered software solutions for businesses that are done wasting time on work that can be automated. We don't sell off-the-shelf tools — we engineer solutions built around your exact problems, your exact workflow, and your exact results.

From lead generation engines that surface 90% qualified prospects with a single click, to automation platforms that handle thousands of tasks while you sleep, to operational systems that turn inbox chaos into data-driven clarity — every solution we ship is designed to create measurable, lasting impact.

Julien

Founder & Lead Engineer, LexAi

Before LexAi, I worked on AI products at Google (Gemini) and Amazon, where I saw firsthand how enterprise-scale automation creates compounding advantages — and how most small and mid-size businesses never get access to those same tools. That gap is what LexAi is built to close.

I built LexAi after spending years watching businesses lose enormous amounts of time and money to processes that should have been automated long ago. Whether it was sales teams grinding through unqualified LinkedIn lists, marketers manually signing up for thousands of newsletters, or IT directors drowning in untracked support requests — the pattern was always the same: the problem was clear, the solution just hadn't been built yet.

That's what we do. We come in, understand the problem precisely, and build the tool that solves it — iterating until the results are exactly right. Every solution we ship is engineered from the ground up, not adapted from a template.
