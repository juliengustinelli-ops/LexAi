⚡

Revenue, EBITDA, EBIT, D&A, CapEx, Net Debt, Cash, Share Count — across up to 5 fiscal years. Margins and growth auto-calculated.

GPT-4o vs Claude — what's the difference?

Both work. The tool currently runs on GPT-4o. Claude (Anthropic) is available as a swap and is the preferred choice for Azure deployments. Pricing is roughly equivalent at this scale — the decision is usually about which provider your compliance team has already cleared.
