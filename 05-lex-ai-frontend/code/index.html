Finance Use Cases

What we build
for financial services teams

Six high-impact use cases — from quick wins to longer-term infrastructure — drawn from actual workflows that you care about.

Modernize Legacy Systems Your firm is probably running critical decisions on spreadsheets nobody fully understands anymore. We document the logic, validate the outputs, and build something your team can trust — and explain to a regulator. e.g. That 10-year-old allocation model your last analyst built — let's make sure it still does what you think it does.
Automate Model Setup & Data Intake Stop rebuilding the same models from scratch. We deploy AI systems that ingest your data — even from messy PDFs — generate a first-pass financial model, and adapt it to your templates. e.g. Analysts focus on reviewing assumptions, not manual setup.
Deploy AI Apps It shouldn't be this hard to build a useful internal AI tool. We scope it tight, build it fast, and put a working product in front of your team — without the enterprise procurement circus. e.g. Query your trade data conversationally instead of writing Python every time.
Unlock Your Internal Data Your historical portfolio data, reports, and documents are an untapped asset. We build a lightweight central data layer and a chat interface so your team can actually use what you've already collected. e.g. Ask a question. Get an answer from your own data — not a generic model.
