Sample Output

What the findings look like

Every finding is severity-rated and includes the specific fix — not just the problem. Here's a sample from a real B2B SaaS audit.

● Critical

GPTBot and ClaudeBot are blocked in robots.txt

The site's robots.txt includes a Disallow: / directive for GPTBot and ClaudeBot — the crawlers that feed ChatGPT and Claude. The site is completely invisible to two of the three largest AI search platforms. Fix: remove the block and add an llms.txt file to guide AI crawlers to priority content. Estimated effort: 30 minutes.

● High Priority

No FAQ or HowTo schema on 14 pages with question-based content

The site has 14 pages with FAQ sections and how-to guides but none have schema markup. Google AI Overviews and Bing Copilot heavily favor structured content for answer boxes. Adding schema to these pages is the highest-leverage quick win available — estimated 2–3 day implementation by any developer.

● Medium Priority

Homepage citability score is 22/100 — no extractable answer blocks

The homepage copy is written in marketing language ("Transform your business") rather than answer-format language. AI models look for self-contained passages that directly answer a question. Rewriting 3–4 key sections in answer format would raise the citability score from 22 to approximately 65.
