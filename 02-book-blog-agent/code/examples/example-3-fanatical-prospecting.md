# Are You Losing Sales Because You're Not Prospecting Enough?

**A book review of "Fanatical Prospecting" by Jeb Blount**

Every salesperson knows the fear of an empty pipeline.

But here's the uncomfortable truth: most sales problems aren't closing problems—they're prospecting problems.

## About the Author

Jeb Blount is the founder of Sales Gravy, one of the most popular sales training platforms in the world. He's written multiple bestsellers on sales performance and is known for his no-nonsense, tactical approach.

## What the Book Covers

"Fanatical Prospecting" strips away excuses and gives you a system for keeping your pipeline full. Here's the framework:

1. **Finding leads through diversified channels** — Phone, email, social, referrals—use them all
2. **Quick qualification based on need, authority, budget, timing** — Stop wasting time on dead ends
3. **Confident closing from a position of abundance** — A full pipeline removes desperation

## Three Big Lessons

✅ **Daily prospecting is non-negotiable.** The top performers treat prospecting like brushing their teeth—it happens every day, no exceptions.

✅ **Clarity in targeting reduces wasted effort.** Know exactly who your ideal customer is before you pick up the phone.

✅ **Continuous prospecting prevents feast-or-famine cycles.** Even when you're busy closing deals, you need to keep filling the top of the funnel.

## Why Read It?

This isn't a book about theory. It's full of scripts, email templates, and outreach strategies you can use right away.

If your pipeline is inconsistent, this book will fix it. But only if you actually do the work.
